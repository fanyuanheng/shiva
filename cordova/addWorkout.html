  • Dumbbell Fly

    The dumbbell fly is and exercise used to stimulate the pectoral muscles in the chest. It is performed by gripping on to two dumbbells (one in each hand), laying back on to a bench, and use the pectoral and deltoid muscles to adduct the weights inwards.
